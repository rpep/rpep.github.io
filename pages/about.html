About Me

Hi, I'm Dr. Ryan Alexander Pepper. I'm a Software Development Engineer at Mentor Graphics, which is part of Siemens Digital Industries Software. I work on the finite-volume method computational fluid dynamics software Flotherm, which is used to model the cooling of electronics devices.

Prior to this, I did my PhD in the Next Generation Computational Modelling Doctoral Training Centre at the University of Southampton, where I studied through simulations the evolution of magnetic systems containing small, topological objects called Skyrmions.

I have a finite Erdos number of 6, through the chain H. Fangohr, S. Cox, D.A. Nicole, E.K. Lloyd, R. J. Wilson, P. Erdos.

I'm a big fan of modern C++, Python, and Linux, and enjoy teaching myself new technologies. I enjoy solving puzzles, and hope to show the solution to some on this site.
